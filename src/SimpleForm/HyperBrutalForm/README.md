# Hyper Brutalism

## Intent
Bold, high-contrast form with stark geometry and punchy feedback.

## Palette
- Primary: Acid yellow/green
- Accent: Hot success tones
- Text: Deep black/white
- Surface: Hard neutral

## Motion
- Typing pulse
- Active glow
- Working spinner
- Success flash + check

## Accessibility
- Maintain legible text at high contrast
- Keep focus rings thick and obvious

## Notes
- Preserve the sharp border radii and heavy drop shadows
