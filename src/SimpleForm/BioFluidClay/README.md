# Bio-Fluid & Clay

## Intent
Organic, tactile form with fluid edges and clay-like depth.

## Palette
- Primary: Teal/aqua
- Accent: Warm success highlights
- Text: Neutral slate
- Surface: Soft clay

## Motion
- Typing pulse
- Active glow
- Working spinner
- Success flash + check

## Accessibility
- Keep text contrast against the clay surface
- Ensure focus styling remains visible on both themes

## Notes
- Preserve the soft shadow stacking and rim lighting
